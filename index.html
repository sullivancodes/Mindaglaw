Minerva & D’Agostino, P.C. was founded approximately 45 years ago by Dominick M. Minerva (1973-2012) and Albert A. D’Agostino in Valley Stream, New York. The Firm’s current partners, Albert A. D’Agostino and Dominick Minerva lead the Firm and are complimented by associates and a talented support staff. The Firm has consistently been on the cutting edge of land use, planning and zoning law and represents major developers of commercial and residential real estate projects throughout Long Island. The firm specializes in appearing before Zoning and Planning Boards, Article 78 proceedings and Landlord/Tenant litigation. The practice includes Trusts and Estates, General Litigation, Labor Law, Business Formation and Municipal representation, including School Districts.
